About Us

The Daily Sniff is a warm and friendly daycare where dogs can enjoy a safe and happy environment while their owners are away. Our team takes care of every dog with love, playtime, and proper rest. We provide spacious play areas, comfortable resting spots, and healthy treats to keep tails wagging all day long.

We also run a special Street Dog Fund where we use donations to feed, vaccinate, and rescue stray dogs. Every small contribution helps us make a big difference. Our goal is to create a caring community for every dog.
